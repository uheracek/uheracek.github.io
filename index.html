Rhythm Forge

Turn any song into a playable 4-key rhythm chart — right in your browser.

♫
Import any audio
Drop in mp3, wav, m4a or ogg files. Everything is decoded locally — your music never leaves your device.
⚙
Smart chart generation
Onset detection, BPM estimation, and energy-driven lane assignment create charts that follow the real rhythm of your music.
★
Four difficulty levels
Easy, Normal, Hard and Insane. Each level adjusts note density, pattern complexity and the chance of double notes.
⚛
Built-in library
Save charts with cached audio, track your best scores and grades, and jump back into any song instantly.
How to play
Notes fall from the top of the screen toward the judgment line. Press the matching key when a note reaches the line.
D   F   J   K   — you can rebind these in Settings.
